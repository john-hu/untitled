White Chocolate and Gingersnap Tiramisu

Lolibox

Read Directions

Description

Ginger spice is a common spice Holiday favorite for most households. It warms the heart on the cold winter nights. This season, make this gingersnap, crunchy and smooth tiramisu as your Holiday time dessert. Sweet mascarpone cheese contrasts the bitter white chocolate flavors. Crunchy biting gingersnap crumbles rest atop a light layered dessert of sweet and spicy exception.

Ingredients

US|METRIC
4 SERVINGS
  • 3 eggs
  • 50 grams sugar
  • 250 grams mascarpone
  • 80 grams white chocolate
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • gingersnaps
  • milk
  • gingersnaps (crumbled)

    Directions

    1. 1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks.
    2. 2. Whisk the yolks with the sugar until it becomes pale. Add the mascarpone and whisk thoroughly.
    3. 3. Melt the white chocolate in the microwave with the water. When it is melted, add it to the previous mixture and mix well.
